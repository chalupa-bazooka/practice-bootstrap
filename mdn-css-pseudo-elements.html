Pseudo elements in CSS

As explained on MDN

::after & ::before

List of clowns:
  • Bozo
  • Krusty
  • Pennywise
  • Ronaldu Macdonaldu

With the help of ::after pseudo-element we've placed the clown emoji after "Krusty". While ::before let us place a burger before "Ronaldu".

::first-letter & ::first-line

The first letter of this paragraph is customized with the help of ::first-letter.

And this paragraph has its entire first line customized with the help of ::first-line. Note that the exact length of the first line varies and depends on the viewport size.

::marker & ::selection

List of dwarves:

  • Smurf
  • Grumpy
  • Driller

Here we've replaced the regular bullets in a list with santa's faces with ::marker. Try highlighting the text on the list and you'll see that it becomes white with red background – it's done with ::selection.

::placeholder & ::file-selector-button

The placeholder in the first input has been customized with ::placeholder. While the button has been customized with ::file-selector-button.
